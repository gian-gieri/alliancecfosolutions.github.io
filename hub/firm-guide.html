The Client Journey

The complete lifecycle — from a stranger finding you on LinkedIn to a client sending you referrals. Seven stages, fully systematized.

1

ATTRACT Get on their radar

  • LinkedIn content (3×/week) drives profile visits
  • Website (alliancecfosolutions.com) converts visitors to calls
  • Referral partners send warm leads
  • 60-person connection list for targeted outreach
linkedin/content-calendar.md linkedin/dashboard-v2.html alliance-cfo/website/index.html linkedin/sales-navigator-60-connections.md
2

CONNECT First conversation

  • Prospect books Free Intro Call (20 min) via Calendly
  • You assess fit in 20 minutes using intro call framework
  • If fit → send Discovery Session link
  • If not fit → graceful pass, refer out
client-systems/calendly-best-practices.md client-systems/calendly-links.md
3

DISCOVER Deep diagnostic

  • Discovery Session (45 min) — follow the script
  • Ask the 7 core questions, listen for pain, diagnose
  • Within 24 hours: send proposal
client-systems/discovery-call-script.md client-systems/follow-up-emails.md
4

PROPOSE Present the solution

  • One-page proposal using template (customized per tier)
  • Follow-up cadence: Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
  • If they say yes → engagement letter
client-systems/proposal-template.md client-systems/follow-up-emails.md
5

ONBOARD Set the foundation

  • Signed engagement letter (Diagnostic / Essential / Growth / Strategic)
  • Welcome email sent with onboarding checklist
  • Collect: QBO access, bank statements, tax returns, org chart, debt schedule
  • Create client folder using standard structure
  • Week 1–4 onboarding timeline
client-systems/engagement-letter-*.md client-systems/welcome-email.md client-systems/onboarding-checklist.md client-systems/client-folder-template.md
6

DELIVER Monthly rhythm

  • Week 1: Books close, reconciliation
  • Week 2: Review, build financial package, update forecast + KPIs
  • Week 3: Deliver package to client 48hrs before call
  • Week 4: Strategy call → summary email → action items
  • Repeat every month
monthly-delivery/monthly-review-checklist.md monthly-delivery/financial-package-template.md monthly-delivery/strategy-call-agenda.md monthly-delivery/call-summary-email.md monthly-delivery/cash-flow-forecast-guide.md monthly-delivery/week-by-week-cadence.md
7

GROW Expand & multiply

  • Quarterly Business Reviews every 90 days
  • Watch for upsell triggers (client needs more)
  • Ask for referrals and testimonials
  • Client becomes referral source
monthly-delivery/quarterly-business-review.md client-systems/pricing-reference.md

Service Tiers

Four tiers designed to meet clients where they are — and grow with them.

Diagnostic

$1,500/mo

60-day engagement

  • Cash flow snapshot
  • Strategic roadmap delivery
  • 2 strategy calls
  • Ongoing monthly support
  • KPI dashboard

Ideal client: Not sure what they need yet. Wants clarity before committing.

↑ Upsell trigger: "I want this every month"

Essential

$2,500/mo

Ongoing engagement

  • Monthly close & reconciliation
  • Financial package delivery
  • 1 strategy call/month
  • Email support
  • Cash flow forecasting
  • KPI tracking

Ideal client: $1–3M revenue, needs financial clarity and a monthly rhythm.

↑ Upsell trigger: "I need help forecasting cash"

Growth

$5,000/mo

Ongoing engagement

  • 13-month cash flow forecast
  • KPI dashboard (industry-specific)

$7,500–10K/mo

Ongoing engagement

  • Board/investor reporting

20 minPUBLIC

When to send: Website CTA, LinkedIn DMs, referral partners

Lead Scoring

ARevenue $1M+, expressed pain, decision-maker, ready to act
BRight profile, some interest, needs nurturing, 30–60 day timeline
CEarly stage, unclear fit, low urgency — track but don't chase

Weekly Pipeline Review

Every Monday morning, 5 minutes:

  1. Review all active deals
  2. Move stale deals forward or out
  3. Identify follow-ups due this week
  4. Check referral partner touchpoints
  5. Update lead scores

Monthly Delivery System

Every month follows the same four-week rhythm. Predictable for you, impressive for clients.

Week 1

Close

Books close, reconciliation, clean up transactions in QBO

Week 2

Build

Review financials, build package, update forecast & KPIs

Week 3

Deliver

Send financial package to client 48hrs before strategy call

Week 4

Strategize

Strategy call → summary email → action items → repeat

What the Client Receives Each Month

  • Financial package (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, AR/AP aging)
  • 13-month rolling cash flow forecast (Growth+)
  • Industry-specific KPI dashboard (Growth+)
  • Strategy call with agenda sent in advance
  • Post-call summary email with action items
